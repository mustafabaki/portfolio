Details About the Project

The project is a travel planner app. The users can search for a country that they want to visit in order to get useful information about the country. In every launch, the app shows random countries so that users can learn about new cultures and countries. On the country information page, users can learn the activities of the country, see the images of the country and find useful information of the country such as population, capital city, spoken languages and its continent.

In addition to that, users can generate travel itinaries and take notes about what to take with them in their lugggage. The application shows the countries that the user can go without a visa.
